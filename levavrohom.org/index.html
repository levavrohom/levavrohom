BEIS MEDRESH LEV AVROHOM is a vibrant and growing Kehillah were the intellectual elite of Ramat Eshkol gather every Shabbos to experience a sublimely spiritual adventure led by our fearless leader R' Yecheskel Weinfeld shlita - a renowned Talmudic scholar and Chassidic mystic who has authored many groundbreaking Seforim on those topics, and has been in various positions of Jewish leadership for close to four decades. The Davening is Nusach ASHKNAZ with a Yeshivah style.
In general the Rov gives a Shiur Friday night between Kabalos Shabbos and Maariv in Lashon Hakodesh. Shabbos morning before Davnening there is a Shiur on Tanya (in english), and after Davening the Rov shares his Haaros and Chidushim on the Parshah over Kiddush (in english)
